sentiment was appropriate saturday night; having a good-bye dinner (on a four year old gift certificate c/o tom landry) with cory at terilli's, i ran into *amber*, who i'd taken home from mick's two years ago, then had seen at the fair six months later... *she* recognized me, and saw ron d. sitting in with the band, and he joined us for a quick catch up.
