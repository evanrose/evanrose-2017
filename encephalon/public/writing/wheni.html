back

when i raised my eyes to say enjoy it
i meant it, i wanted you
to be looking back, and you were
it happened twice
i had to catch my breath
that moment was timeless
we were together we were one
we were, forever, in a glance
butterflies in my stomach
maybe i recognized you
in a past life you may have
held a special place in my harem
you may have been a shark
attacking my peaceful school
i might have been a mother
bringing you into this world
seen in one unique way for
one unique moment and most likely
never seen that way again
i've seen you at the store
ahead of me in line
passing in traffic
from a window
and wanted you, never knowing
how to get to you
but there you are, again
not to see me, but i am here
did you feel it? do you remember?
i think maybe you did
and you knew my name
