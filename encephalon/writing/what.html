What is it exactly that i want?
does it have to be exact?
i desire freedom from pain and responsibility
yet i accept them both with open arms
i want to see feel and know all
yet i get comfortable in my now.
i want to go and stay
i want to wander and be grounded
i want to know and feel you.
but i am myself.
life is too short, yet i have
so much time to kill
why was i put here of all places?
do i have a purpose to fulfill?
do i serve myself or all mankind?
is there a happy medium?
do i search for it, or accept
how things are?
does the world belong to me
do i belong to the world
i want to be secure in my decisions
happy with my risks
true to myself
one with all
what is it exactly that i want?
i want to be happy
i want to be me.
