back

i watch her standing
silouetted by the rising sun
next to a window facing east
she stands there naked
turning her head to face me
hand on her curving hip
a lock of hair falling
i see her mischevous look
and i smile
she comes to me
and i accept her
taking her in my arms
loving her
thinking of dinner, laughter, music
thinking of dancing, drinking
walking hand in hand
she takes me
she moves me
she loves me more than i can take
like rivers flowing to the sea
i flow into her
parting her filling her
giving myself to her
becoming one with her
knowing i'll never be the same
