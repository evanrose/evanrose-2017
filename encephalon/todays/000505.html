8:54 AM 5/5/00 (6 days 'til may 11!)

coming down to the wire, baby.

so i'm packed, got all my bills paid, picking up checks, kicking out work, right up until the very last; its amazing to me still how rightly all of this has come together. its next to perfect.
perfect, except i haven't heard word on my new place, and except for rolling out to metro coffee group to pick up a check and having a metal rod (or something) get kicked up into my car that not only broke my entire windshield, but put a cute little dent on the roof. oh man. so after a bit of raging and an after rage calm, i got to finally speak to them about all the crazy shit that went down on easter (apparently some sort of big misunderstanding).

anyway, highs and lows, whatever. this is obviously (even if only obvious to me) that these are the things that are supposed to be happening, and things are how they should be.
over a pre-mother's day dinner of steaks and good conversation, i remembered this...

and i guess (after staring at this page awhile) that is all that matters.
