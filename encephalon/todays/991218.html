12-18-99

thank god in heaven. my laptop is working again. it seems as though a little tape can fix anything! this means that the new site is on its way, also a good thing, because then i'll be able to obsess on another site... my second night at a new job starts in 20 minutes, i'm working for cory again; though i don't feel terribly good about it. my time is more valuable than it used to be.

i've bumped into 3 old friends today, and making holiday plans. i love christmastime in a college town. finally, thank you to cory and stephen for the deal that went through yesterday. i only wish it could have happened sooner. merry christmas!
