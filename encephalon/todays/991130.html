montreal was fabulous, daria was wonderful, a great time was had by all. after going out dancing 4 out of the five nights i was there, i have officially become a clubkid. oddly, (or not) i came home feeling sad after being reminded that things had not worked out like i had wanted them to (a long story), but there always seems to be more to life when they don't.

i hit the ground running today, and shit seems to be blowing up all over the place, i.e. job offers, everybody's starting something, and everybody wants me to be a part of it, it feels great to be back in bidness, though thank heaven for this vacation. good things are happening to everyone, which is wonderful too.
