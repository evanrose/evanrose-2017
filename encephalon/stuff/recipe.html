picnic recipe

what you'll need:

1 loaf of freshly baked and sliced bread
1 package of pesto
1 hunk of real (restaurant quality) mozzerella cheese
6 roma tomatos
$10.00 worth (in season value) smoked atlantic salmon
2 bottles of water
1 bottle of white wine (if one happens to drink)
1 (or more) friend(s)
1 beautiful day (preferably towards the end)
1 (or more) beautiful view(s)
and plenty of plastic/silverware, napkins, and a winekey.

first, enjoy a beautiful day with friends, get really hungry, then drive to a beautiful spot of your choice. get comfy, then spread pesto, cheese slices, tomato slices onto the bread. pile salmon on top, stuff your face, and enjoy good conversation.

repeat as soon as possible.
